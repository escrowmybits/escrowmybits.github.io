Types of Escrow

Regular Escrow

You deposit your funds into a bitcoin address controlled by us and the funds are forwarded to cold storage

Regular Escrow pegged to fiat value

You deposit into a bitcoin address controlled by Coinapult and Coinapult pegs the value to a fiat or asset value

Mutli-Signature Escrow

Your funds are kept in a secure 2 of 3 multi-signature transaction where we hold only one key

Multi-Signature Escrow pegged to a fiat value

Your funds are kept in a secure 2 of 3 multi-signature transaction using Omni(mastercoin) or Nubits to peg the value
